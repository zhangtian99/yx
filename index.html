About the Game

Welcome to Survival Race, a fast-paced, action-packed running game designed to test your agility and reflexes. In this survivor race game, you control a character who must navigate through a series of increasingly difficult obstacle courses. Your goal in this race survival challenge is simple: survive the race for as long as you can, collect points, and set a new high score. It's easy to learn but challenging to master this survival race game!

How to Play

The controls in our race survival game are intuitive. Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or on-screen controls on mobile) to move left and right, and press the spacebar (or tap the screen) to jump over hurdles, gaps, and other dangerous traps. Timing is everything. A single misstep could mean the end of your run, so stay focused and anticipate the obstacles ahead to survive the race.

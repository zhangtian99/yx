Survival Race

The ultimate online obstacle race. Run, jump, and conquer the course to prove you're the best!

About the Game

Welcome to Survival Race, a fast-paced, action-packed running game designed to test your agility and reflexes. In this game, you control a character who must navigate through a series of increasingly difficult obstacle courses. Your goal is simple: survive as long as you can, collect points, and set a new high score. It's easy to learn but challenging to master!

How to Play

The controls are intuitive. Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or on-screen controls on mobile) to move left and right, and press the spacebar (or tap the screen) to jump over hurdles, gaps, and other dangerous traps. Timing is everything. A single misstep could mean the end of your run, so stay focused and anticipate the obstacles ahead.

Game Features

- **Endless Fun:** The course is procedurally generated, meaning you get a new, unique challenge every time you play.
- **Responsive Controls:** Whether you're on a PC or a mobile device, the controls are smooth and responsive.
- **Clean Graphics:** Enjoy a minimalist and clear art style that lets you focus on the action.
- **Leaderboards:** Compete with players from around the world to see who is the true Survival Race champion. (Feature coming soon!)
